Thermocouple Device Documentation¶

class sensors.Thermocouple¶

Tango Device for a thermocouple.

Device states description:
  • DevState.ON: equipment ok
  • DevState.DISABLED: equipment disabled
  • DevState.ALARM: equipment interlocked
  • DevState.FAULT: communication error
